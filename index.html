Climate Change in
Taiwan

The problem

The problem Taiwan, a country of quick infrastructure over the years and a booming economy is all great until it interferes with the country's climate and ecosystem. Taiwan has had issues over the years, from its habitat and water systems all being compromised due to the lack of care for the environment. The water systems take a big hit and have the most important impact on people's lives in Taiwan. They only have their own water supplies being that they're an island

Acid Rain

Acid rain is a byproduct of climate change. It is rain that has lower pH levels compared to normal. This rain has an effect on so many things; From quicker decay of buildings and objects that encounter it, acid rain also creates issues with the air as it pollutes the air and releases small particles that can cause respiratory problems for sensitive people and could create new problems for others. Crops are at risk due to acid rain rice and its a product that is losing its nutritional value due to the decreased pH levels of the soil.

Flooding

Flooding is having a massive impact on Taiwan. Typhoons transpire more often due to the rising global temperatures. This happens due to trapped greenhouse gasses that are released into the earth's atmosphere and affecting the weather cycles. Flooding is a more visual tragedy, it's not something hiding in the air and damaging our lives slowly. Flooding destroys all advances of humans from destroying homes and businesses to destroying and killing crops, livestock, and humans. All these are happening more and more often due to our climate change issues this makes rebuilding a hassle and costly.
